About Us

Medicare is a an online clinic where patients are able to interact with our specialized doctors at the comfort of their home. The patients are only required to fill out the symptoms in an online form one can still access the clinic physically since we direct our patients straight to where the specific medical service, that is required is offered. We have branches all over the country and our emergency response team are trained to quickly capture patient details and respond in shortest time. We provide amazing and affordable services. We also keep our patients records as there is an electronic database assigned to Medicare-Online
